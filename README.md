# threadlocked

threadlocked is an invite-only community for the kind of people who accidentally spend twelve hours reversing a binary because something about it felt wrong.

we are aggressively elitist by design.

the average member here is autismmaxxed beyond repair. reverse engineering proprietary software, reading compiler output for fun, staring at packet captures at 4am and spending an entire weekend chasing a single bug is considered normal behavior.

most online tech communities are full of tourists. people who like the aesthetic of being technical. people who collect certificates, grind leetcode, post linkedin slop and call themselves engineers.

we are interested in the tiny minority who can actually build things, break things and understand things.

threadlockers has an unusually high concentration of cracked programmers, hackers, reverse engineers and general computer retards. the kind of people who treat firmware dumps, assembly listings and obscure protocol documentation as recreational reading.

credentials mean nothing.

job titles mean nothing.

follower counts mean nothing.

the work speaks for itself.

entry is invite-only.

if your first instinct is to ask for an invite, you probably aren't getting one.

if you're genuinely cracked, we might accept an application.

all members are part of the threadlocked webring:

https://ring.seggs.lol

the internet spent twenty years lowering the bar.

we spent twenty years stepping over it.
